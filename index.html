Expert Advice

Tips for Healthy Skin

🧼
Never Sleep with Makeup
Always cleanse your face before bed to prevent clogged pores and breakouts.
✨
Active Ingredients
Apply toner & serum 2-3 times weekly for best results without irritation.
💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 2L of water daily to keep your skin plump and glowing.
🥗
Eat Clean
Fill your plate with fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins for healthy skin.
☀️
Sunscreen Daily
Apply SPF every morning - even on cloudy days - to prevent premature aging.
🛌
Beauty Sleep
Get 7-8 hours of quality sleep for optimal skin repair and regeneration.
❄️
Cold Water Rinse
Rinse your face with cold water after cleansing to tighten pores and boost circulation.
🧴
Double Cleanse
Use oil-based cleanser first, then water-based for a deep, thorough clean.
🔄
Change Pillowcases
Change your pillowcase twice weekly to prevent bacteria buildup and breakouts.
❌
Don't Touch Your Face
Avoid touching your face throughout the day to prevent transferring dirt and bacteria.
